His Love Ministries

A 501(c) 3 non-profit Christian Ministry

WE LOVE THE UNLOVED THROUGH OUR NURSING
HOME, PRISON, AND RADIO MINISTRY

His Love Ministries was originated by God placing a burden on one man's heart, basically with a few simple verses from the Bible.

- Matthew 25:35-40 - For I was hungry and you gave Me food; I was thirsty and you gave Me drink; I was a stranger and you took Me in; 36 I was naked and you clothed Me; I was sick and you visited Me; I was in prison and you came to Me.' 37 "Then the righteous will answer Him, saying, `Lord, when did we see You hungry and feed You, or thirsty and give You drink? 38 `When did we see You a stranger and take You in, or naked and clothe You? 39 Or when did we see You sick, or in prison, and come to You?' 40 "And the King will answer and say to them, `Assuredly, I say to you, inasmuch as you did it to one of the least of these My brethren, you did it to Me".

- James 1:27 - Pure and undefiled religion before God and the Father is this: to visit orphans and widows in their trouble, and to keep oneself unspotted from the world.

HLM conducts hundreds of church services and Bible studies yearly, in and around the Savannah, Ga area, in various Nursing Homes, Assisted Living Facilities, Prisons, Jails, Transitional Centers, Children's Centers, and Homeless Centers. To see our full schedule please go to the "Services" Page.

The goal of HLM is to spread the gospel to those who really need to hear it and equip those who know Christ as Lord and Savior to minister to others. We reach out to those the World has forgotten.

Please take a moment to pray and considering helping our ministry where God leads you. We always need your prayer support. If you are interested in volunteering, please contact us at (912) 398-0573. If you are interested in giving, please see our "Contact Us" page for information on giving, or simply click on the "Donate Now Through Network for Good Button" in the upper right corner of this page.

For more information about our ministry, please navigate around our web site, and feel free to contact us with any questions.
